The department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Barishal (CSEBU), in collaboration with the Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC) aims to offer various skilled development courses to prepare smart citizens in Bangladesh.

  • Training Kits will be provided
  • Refreshment facilities
  • Certificate after completing courses
